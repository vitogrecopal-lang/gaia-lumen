# Gaia-Lumen: short cover letter

Subject: preliminary review request - Gaia-Lumen / Epsilon Eridani model

Dear colleagues,

we are submitting Gaia-Lumen, a digital interdisciplinary modeling project that organizes astronomical data, habitability parameters, interstellar communication scenarios and long-term biological-continuity hypotheses.

The project does not claim the discovery of a new planet. The name Galia-Lumen refers to a modeled planet placed, for simulation purposes, within the estimated habitable zone of the Epsilon Eridani system.

The attached documentation explicitly separates:

- confirmed astronomical data;
- values derived from standard formulas;
- model parameters;
- speculative or project-level elements.

We would appreciate, if possible, preliminary guidance on three points:

1. the consistency of the astronomical parameters used for Epsilon Eridani;
2. the physical plausibility of the modeled planet and its habitability parameters;
3. suitable scientific channels, archives or authorized procedures for any future review of the information package.

No unauthorized radio transmission has been made. Any future real external communication would be considered only through authorized channels, appropriate licensing and scientific review.

Main documents:

- GAIA_LUMEN_PUBLIC_BRIEF.html
- GAIA_LUMEN_DOSSIER_SCIENTIFICO_BREVE.pdf
- GAIA_LUMEN_BIBLIOGRAFIA_PARAMETRI.md

Thank you for your attention and for any guidance you may be able to provide.

Sincerely,

Gaia-Lumen Project
